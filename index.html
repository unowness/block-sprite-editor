Blockmode
ASCII Sprite Editor

Draw block-ASCII sprites in four colors (body + accent + decor + background). Glyphs sit edge-to-edge so block and box lines connect. Mirror, Undo / Redo, Resize and an Animation timeline are built in. Export or Load JSON to reuse sprites anywhere. Export your art in SVG, PNG or MP4 formats with up to ×16 upscale.
